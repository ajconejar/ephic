Our Company

Ellestreque Pharmaceutical Industrial Corporation

History

With over 24 years of marketing knowledge and experience, Ellestreque Pharmaceutical Industrial Corporation has been serving the market since January 1987. Initially, we marketed cosmetics imported from China and Taiwan throughout Davao and Cotabato provinces. Starting with just one employee, we booked product orders in target supermarkets and drug stores, using passenger buses and rented vans for delivery. Over time, our operations expanded to Mindanao provinces, including Agusan, Surigao, and Bukidnon.

Background

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Ensure the achievement of sales targets.
  • Responsible for timely and proper display and replenishment of merchandise.
  • Receive and check the accuracy of all product deliveries and report discrepancies immediately.
  • Conduct inventory as scheduled.
  • Identify and segregate items for pull-out, damage, dirt, and slow-moving items.
  • Safeguard merchandise from external and internal losses (e.g., B.O, damage).
  • Maintain orderliness and cleanliness in the selling and stockroom areas.
  • Prepare and submit accurate reports on time.
  • Provide fast, efficient, and courteous service to customers at all times.

MISSION

Ellestreque Pharmaceutical and Industrial Corporation aims to create affordable pharmaceutical products and deliver quality products throughout the country. We participate in the mission of major business corporations to achieve maximum utility while providing genuine service to clients. Additionally, we contribute to Filipino communities by offering beneficial working opportunities and environments.

VISION

Ellestreque Pharmaceutical and Industrial Corporation is a newly-founded business corporation equipped with marketing knowledge and experience. We are responsive to the needs and challenges of the economy through our work in pharmaceutical product manufacturing and distribution to meet market demands.
