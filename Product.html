Hypochlorox (Liquid Bleach)

DIRECTION FOR USE:

Laundry: Seperate laundry by color and fabric. Wash whites separates from other colors. Do not use on silk, wool, and leather. Pre-treat heavy soils and dark stains, Use 5 Tablespoons of HYPOCHLOROX per 1 gallon of water and soak laundry for 5-10 minutes. Add detergent. Wash clothes and rinse well.

For machine wash:

Dilute 1 tablespoon of HYPOCHLOROX to a 1 liter of water, add detergent and complete the wash cycle after soaking laundry for 10 minutes. Rinse well.

For household use:

Mix 3 tablespoon HYPOCHLOROX to a liter of water. Spread the solution to the surfaces and allow 5-10 minutes to contact before rinsing.
